Synthetic monitoring to enable automatic outage mitigation

Citrix 2021

As part of an effort to replace our manual outage mitigation process with an automated system I created Synthetic Monitoring scripts. We ran these scripts via New relic against our production and internal environments to create live continuous E2E testing and alerting. This alerting was consumed by a health monitoring service that could respond to outages in specific synthetics by changing traffic manager rules to route traffic away from dysfunctional instances. The automatic outage resolution effort as a whole brought my team's owned services from 99.9% to 99.985% uptime.
